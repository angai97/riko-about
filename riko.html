Riko
from Made in Abyss

About Riko

Riko is the main character of the anime and manga series, Made in Abyss, where cave raiders collect treasures and discover the unknown in the gaping chasm in the center of the town of Orth, even at the cost of their lives. Riko, along with her close companions, ventures into the Abyss in search of her mother, who may be living in the 7th layer.

  • Riko is 12 years old and grew up in an orphanage in the town of Orth
  • Her mother is a legendary White Whistle, the highest rank of Abyss cave raiders
  • Riko is a curious, optimistic, and smol child that must be protected at all costs

Riko's Precious People

Growing up in an orphange, Riko became close with a few of the kids her age, but is known for causing trouble for her teachers and caregivers because of her overly enthusiastic and adventurous tendencies. Below are a few people Riko holds dear to her heart!

Lyza

Lyza, a.k.a "The Annihilator," is a legendary White Whistle Abyss delver who is known for her fiery personality and tremendous achievements raiding the chasm. Riko keeps her mother's notes and uses them when she ventures into the Abyss.

Jiruo

Jiruo is a teacher and caretaker at Riko's orphanage. As someone who grew up admiring Lyza, Riko's mother, Jiruo, though normally serious and strict, is one of Riko's greatest supporters.

Reg

Reg is a humanoid robot with origins from the Abyss. Riko first meets Reg while on an excursion to the 1st layer of the Abyss, and are inseparable since.

Nanachi

Nanachi is a Narehate, a human who survived ascending from the 6th layer of the Abyss. Riko and Reg are saved by Nanachi during their fight against a mpowerful beast in 4th layer of the Abyss.
